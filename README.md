# Health-Environment-Data-Linking

All things health and environmental data linking. So, stuff from my PhD, fun projects to learn. 

For example, the recent flash floods in Kenya were devastating. This has happened before, and it appears that the crisis response was not properly anticipated. I thought it would be interesting to find sources of flood data from years and compare if these issues keep happening in the same locations and at what magnitude. I thought it would also be interesting to explore the sources of historical and more recent flood datasets and to assess their usability for crisis management and predictive analysis. 
The work is being done in this notebook: https://github.com/karegapauline/Health-Environment-Data-Linking/blob/main/flood_dashboard.ipynb
